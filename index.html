Who We Are

ModuForge 3D is a small-batch, locally owned studio specialising in functional FDM prints designed for real-world desk and home use. Every product is tested in-house before it ships.

We use high-quality PLA+ and PETG filaments, precision-tuned for dimensional accuracy. No warping, no rough edges — just clean, repeatable parts that last.
